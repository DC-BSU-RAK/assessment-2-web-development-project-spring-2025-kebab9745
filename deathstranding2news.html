Death Stranding 2: On The Beach Announced

Posted on December 9, 2022

Hideo Kojima and Kojima Productions have officially announced Death Stranding 2: On The Beach. The sequel to the groundbreaking and genre-defying Death Stranding, promises to expand upon its unique world and gameplay.

What We Know So Far:

  • Returning Characters: Norman Reedus returns as Sam Porter Bridges, and other familiar faces are expected to make an appearance.
  • Expanded World: The story will take place in a new location.
  • New Threats: Sam will face new challenges and enemies on his journey.
  • Evolving Gameplay: Kojima Productions is expected to introduce new gameplay mechanics.

Kojima's Vision:

Hideo Kojima has described the game as not a direct sequel, but something new.

Release Date:

A release date has not been announced yet, but the game is currently in development.
