Cyberpunk 2077

Description:

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world, action-adventure RPG set in the megalopolis of Night City, where you play as V, a mercenary outlaw going after a one-of-a-kind implant that is the key to immortality. With cyberware modifications, you can customize your playstyle as you take on jobs, build a reputation, and unlock upgrades. The choices you make and the paths you take will shape the story and the world around you.

Details:

  • Genre: Action RPG, Open World
  • Setting: The vibrant and dangerous metropolis of Night City, California, in the year 2077
  • Platforms: PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4, Xbox Series X|S, Xbox One, PC
  • Release Date: December 10, 2020
  • Developer: CD Projekt Red
  • Publisher: CD Projekt
  • Art Style: A blend of futuristic and gritty aesthetics, with towering skyscrapers, neon-lit streets, and cybernetic enhancements.
  • Key Features:
    • A non-linear story with branching narratives and multiple endings
    • Extensive character customization through cyberware and skill trees
    • A vast and immersive open world to explore, with diverse districts and secrets to uncover
    • Engaging combat with a variety of weapons, cyberware abilities, and combat styles
    • Meaningful choices that impact the story and relationships with other characters
    • A mature and complex narrative exploring themes of transhumanism, identity, and mortality
    • Iconic characters and memorable storylines
    • Post-launch support including updates, free DLCs, and expansions like Phantom Liberty
