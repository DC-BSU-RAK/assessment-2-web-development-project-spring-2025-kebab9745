Cyberpunk 2077

Initial Release Issues

Cyberpunk 2077's launch was plagued by bugs, performance problems, and missing features, especially on consoles. This led to widespread criticism and damaged CD Projekt Red's reputation.

Redemption Through Updates

Years of patches and updates have significantly improved the game. Performance is better, many bugs have been fixed, and promised features have been added.

Current Status

Cyberpunk 2077 is now generally well-received, particularly on PC and current-gen consoles. The "Phantom Liberty" expansion has further boosted its reputation. While the initial launch is still a sore point, the game is now considered a worthwhile experience by many.

God of War Ragnarok

Critical and Commercial Success

God of War Ragnarok was highly praised by critics and a commercial success. It continues the story of Kratos and Atreus in Norse mythology.

Narrative and Gameplay Excellence

The game is celebrated for its strong narrative, emotional depth, and excellent performances. Its combat system is refined and engaging, and its world is beautiful and immersive.

Awards and Legacy

Ragnarok won numerous awards, including Game of the Year at The Game Awards 2022. It's considered a standout title in the series and one of the best games of its generation.

Horizon Forbidden West

Positive Reception

Horizon Forbidden West was well-received upon release. It expands on the world of Horizon Zero Dawn, following Aloy's journey.

Stunning World and Improved Gameplay

The game features a large, visually impressive open world and builds upon the gameplay of its predecessor with new mechanics and enemy types.

Narrative and Themes

The game's story explores themes of environmentalism and the impact of past actions. Aloy's character development and the supporting cast are strong.
