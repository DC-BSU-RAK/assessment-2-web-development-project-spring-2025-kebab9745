Horizon Forbidden West

Description:

Embark on Aloy's next grand adventure in Horizon Forbidden West. Explore a majestic yet dangerous frontier as she seeks to uncover the source of a mysterious and deadly blight that threatens all life. Journey through vast landscapes, encounter awe-inspiring machines and new tribes, and unravel the secrets of the ancient past to save the future of the world.

Details:

  • Genre: Action RPG, Open World
  • Setting: The diverse and dangerous frontier of the Forbidden West
  • Platforms: PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4, PC
  • Release Date: February 18, 2022 (PlayStation), March 21, 2024 (PC)
  • Developer: Guerrilla Games
  • Publisher: Sony Interactive Entertainment
  • Art Style: Stunningly realistic environments with intricate machine designs and vibrant tribal cultures.
  • Key Features:
    • Compelling narrative continuing Aloy's story
    • Massive open world to explore with diverse biomes
    • Thrilling combat against a variety of new and returning machines
    • Engaging interactions with new and established tribes
    • Deep crafting and upgrade systems
    • Exploration of underwater environments
    • Use of new tools and abilities, like the Pullcaster and Shieldwing
    • Unraveling the mysteries of the Old Ones and the Far Zenith
    • Aloy's personal growth and the development of her relationships
