Horizon Forbidden West Expansion Announced

Posted on June 12, 2024

Great news for fans of Horizon Forbidden West! A new expansion has been announced, continuing Aloy's journey in the post-apocalyptic world. The expansion, titled "Burning Shores", will introduce a new region, new characters, and new threats.

What to Expect:

  • New Region: Explore the ruins of modern-day Los Angele, a new region with unique environments and challenges.
  • New Storyline: Continue Aloy's quest as she uncovers the secrets of the Forbidden West and faces a new threat to the planet.
  • New Machines: Encounter new and dangerous machines, each with its own set of behaviors and weaknesses.
  • New Weapons and Skills: Aloy will gain access to new weapons and skills to help her survive in this dangerous world.

Story Details:

The expansion's story will focus on Aloy south to the treacherous, volcano-ridden archipelago that's emerged from the ruins of modern-day Los Angele. Aloy will encounter new allies and enemies as she delves deeper into the mysteries of the past and fights to save the future.

Release Date:

The expansion is set to release on January 5, 2026.
