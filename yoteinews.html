Yotei Region Expansion Coming Soon!

Posted on January 15, 2025

Exciting news for all adventurers! The Yotei region, a vast and unexplored territory, will soon be available in the game. Get ready to embark on a new journey filled with thrilling quests, unique monsters, and valuable treasures.

Key Features:

  • New Quests: Dozens of new quests, including main story quests and side missions.
  • Unique Environments: Explore diverse landscapes, from snowy mountains to volcanicActive Fields.
  • New Monsters: Battle new challenging foes with unique abilities and attack patterns.
  • Powerful Gear: Discover and acquire powerful new weapons, armor, and accessories.
  • Level Cap Increase: Reach new heights with an increased level cap.

Story Teaser:

The Yotei region has been shrouded in mystery for centuries. Legends speak of ancient civilizations and powerful artifacts hidden within its borders. Uncover the secrets of Yotei and determine the fate of this forgotten land.

Release Date:

The Yotei Region Expansion is scheduled to release on February 28, 2025. Stay tuned for more updates and sneak peeks in the coming weeks!
