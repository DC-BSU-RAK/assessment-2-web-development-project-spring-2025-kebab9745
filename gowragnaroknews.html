God of War Ragnarok: Valhalla DLC Announced

Posted on December 8, 2023

Santa Monica Studio has announced a new DLC for God of War Ragnarok: Valhalla. This DLC expands the God of War Ragnarok story

What's New:

  • New Storyline: A new epilogue story that expands the God of War Ragnarok narrative.
  • New Combat: New combat mechanics
  • New locations: Explore new locations within the realms.
  • New challenges: Face new enemies.

Valhalla Synopsis:

Kratos travels to a mysterious new destination, Valhalla, with Mimir. There he will face challenges that will test him.

Release Date:

The Valhalla DLC is scheduled to release on December 12, 2025.
