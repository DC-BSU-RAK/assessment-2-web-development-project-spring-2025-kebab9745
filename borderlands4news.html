Get Ready, Vault Hunters! Borderlands 4 Officially Announced!

Posted on May 13, 2025

The mayhem is returning! Gearbox has finally announced the development of Borderlands 4, the next installment in the iconic looter-shooter franchise. Get ready to dive back into a world of over-the-top action, insane weapons, and unforgettable characters.

What We Know So Far:

  • New Vault Hunters: Expect a brand-new cast of Vault Hunters, each with their own unique skill trees, abilities, and playstyles.
  • A New World: The story will take place on a new planet, promising fresh environments, enemies, and challenges.
  • More Guns Than Ever: Borderlands is known for its insane arsenal, and Borderlands 4 will take it to the next level with even more procedurally generated weaponry.
  • Co-op Mayhem: Team up with your friends for four-player co-op action.

Story Details:

While specific story details are still under wraps, the announcement trailer hints at a new threat to the Borderlands universe. The Vault Hunters will need to band together to stop this new enemy and uncover the secrets of ancient alien technologies.

Release Date:

Gearbox has not yet announced a specific release date, but they have confirmed that the game is in active development. Stay tuned for more updates in the coming months.

What Fans Are Saying:

The announcement has been met with overwhelming excitement from fans. Social media is buzzing with speculation about the new Vault Hunters, the story, and the new features. Many fans are eager to see the return of their favorite characters from previous Borderlands games.
