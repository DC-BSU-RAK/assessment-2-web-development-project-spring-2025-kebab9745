Red Dead Redemption Remaster Announced

Posted on October 26, 2024

Rockstar Games has announced a remastered version of the critically acclaimed Red Dead Redemption. This new version will bring the classic open-world Western to a new generation of consoles and PC, with updated visuals and gameplay enhancements.

What's Included:

  • Updated Visuals: The game will feature enhanced textures, improved lighting, and increased resolution.
  • Performance Boost: Expect smoother gameplay with higher frame rates on supported platforms.
  • Modern Controls: The game will include optional modern control schemes.
  • Undead Nightmare: The Undead Nightmare expansion will be included.

Platforms:

Red Dead Redemption Remaster will be available on PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC.

Release Date:

The remastered version is scheduled to release on February 15, 2026.
