Mafia: Lost Heaven Ray Tracing Update

Posted on July 14, 2024

A new update for Mafia: Lost Heaven adds ray tracing support, enhancing the visuals of the classic game's remake. This update brings improved lighting and reflections to the world of Lost Heaven.

Key Features:

  • Ray-Traced Reflections: See more realistic reflections on surfaces throughout the city.
  • Ray-Traced Global Illumination: Experience improved lighting.
  • Performance Options: Players can customize the ray tracing settings to balance visual quality and performance.

How to Update:

The update is available now for the PC version of Mafia: Lost Heaven. Players can download it through their respective game launchers (Steam, Epic Games Store).

Initial Impressions:

Early reports suggest that the ray tracing update significantly improves the game's atmosphere, adding a new layer of immersion to the experience. The improved lighting and reflections bring the city to life in a way that wasn't possible before.
