Killing Floor 3 Officially Announced

Posted on August 24, 2024

The highly anticipated sequel, Killing Floor 3, has been officially announced! Tripwire Interactive has unveiled the next installment in the popular co-op survival horror franchise, promising to deliver even more intense Zed-slaying action.

What We Know So Far:

  • Return of the Zeds: The genetically engineered horrors are back in greater numbers and with new, terrifying variations.
  • Co-op Mayhem: Team up with friends to take on waves of Zeds in adrenaline-pumping, cooperative combat.
  • New Weapons and Tools: A variety of new weapons and tools will be available to help players dismember and destroy the Zed menace.
  • Enhanced Gore System: Killing Floor 3 will feature an even more advanced gore system, delivering visceral and satisfying Zed destruction.

Developer Insights:

Tripwire Interactive has emphasized their commitment to delivering the intense, challenging, and highly replayable experience that fans of the series have come to expect. They are also focusing on [Developer Focus].

Release Date:

While an exact release date has not been announced, Killing Floor 3 is expected to release in July 24 , 2025 . More information will be revealed in the coming months.
