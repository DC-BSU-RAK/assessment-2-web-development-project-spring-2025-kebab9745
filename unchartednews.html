New Uncharted Game in Development

Posted on October 26, 2024

Exciting news for fans of the Uncharted series! A new Uncharted game is reportedly in development. While details are scarce, rumors suggest that Naughty Dog is at the helm of the project, and it may explore a new chapter in the Uncharted universe.

Rumored Details:

  • New Protagonist?: There is speculation that the game may feature a new protagonist, though Nathan Drake could make an appearance.
  • Setting: Rumors point to a South America location.
  • New Gameplay Mechanics: The new game may introduce new gameplay mechanics to enhance exploration and combat.

Studio Speculation:

Naughty Dog is rumored to be developing the new Uncharted title. Naughty Dog is known for their work on Last of Us.

Release Window:

The game is still in early development, so a release date is likely a few years away. However, fans are eagerly awaiting more information.
